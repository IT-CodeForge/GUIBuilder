Windows 9x - path settings



Click 'start' and the 'execute'. A window is shown up, which is called the 'DOS-Box'. Here you have to enter the following:

PATH=c:\jdk1.4\BIN;%PATH%

Now the path variable is advanced with our needed path. It's important to keep in mind that c:\jdk1.4 is only an example, you have to use instead your jdk install directory.
