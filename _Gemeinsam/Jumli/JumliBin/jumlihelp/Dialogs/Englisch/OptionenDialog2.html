JDK-Options Dialog




    1. Here you have to enter the path on which the JDK is to be found. Normally Jumli should find this path automatically.

    2. Choose the path here.

    3. Here you have to enter the path where the JDK-Documentation can be found.

    4. Choose the path here.

    5. Here you have to enter the JDK class-overview.

    6. Here you have to enter the JDK package-overview.

    7. Here you have to enter the subdirectory.

    8. You leave the dialog and store the entered data.

    9. To leave the dialog without storing press 'Cancel'.

    10. Here you come into the dialogue help, in whom you are for the moment.






    1. Included Jar-Files will appear here.

    2. Press this button to add jarfiles to the current project. An explorer dialog will appear to let you choose your jarfile.

    3. Press delete to remove the jarfile selected in the listbox above. The jarfile will be removed from the current project.

    4. Click "OK" to exit and save your changes.

    5. Click "Cancel" to exit without saving your changes.

    6. This button leads you to the dialog help where you are right now.
