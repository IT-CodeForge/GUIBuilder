Package Dialog




    1. Enter the name of the package here.

    2. Here you can allocate a stereotype.

    3. To create a new diagram press this button.

    4. Here you come back to the main diagram.

    5.In order to remove the current package. If you pressed 'Del', you must click afterwards 'OK' in order to delete the Use Case absolutely. However this only works, if all classes and diagrams were deleted before.

    6. Here you come into the Docu-Dialog.

    7. Here you come into the dialogue help, in whom you are for the moment.

    8. Here you leave the dialog and store the entered data.

    9. To leave the dialog without storing click 'Cancel'.
