Activity Dialog




    1. Enter the name of the activity here.

    2. Click here to remove the current activity. After pressing 'Delete' you have to confirm with "OK". Now you will be asked if you're sure that you're sure, and if you are, the activity is deleted.

    3.Clicking this button will lead you to the docu-dialog.

    4. This button leads directly to the dialog-help where you are right now.

    5. Click here to exit and save your changes.

    6. Click here to exit without saving your changes.
