Diagram Dialog




    1. Enter the name of the diagram here.

    2. Here you can choose wheter you want to create class-, sequence-, Use Case-, collaborations-, activity- oder state diagrams.

    3. In order to remove the current diagram. If you pressed 'Del', you must click afterwards 'OK' in order to delete the diagram absolutely.

    4. Here you come into the Docu-Dialog.

    5. Here you come into the dialogue help, in whom you are for the moment.

    6. Here you leave the dialog and store the entered data.

    7. To leave the dialog without storing click 'Cancel'.
