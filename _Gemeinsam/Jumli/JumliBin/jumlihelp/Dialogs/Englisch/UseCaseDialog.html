Use Case Dialog





1. Enter the name of the Use Case here.

2. In order to remove the current Use Case. If you pressed 'Del', you must click afterwards 'OK' in order to delete the Use Case absolutely.

3. Here you come into the Docu-Dialog.

4. Here you come into the dialogue help, in whom you are for the moment.

5. Here you leave the dialog and store the entered data.

6. To leave the dialog without storing click 'Cancel'.
